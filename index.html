The issue that #Xcode doesn't want to install on device is a little better in 16.1, but still happens. Found a way to address it without restart though:

1. Cancel the "download" of the app on device.
2. Kill `CoreDeviceService` on the Mac.
3. Delete the app from the device.

That sequence has worked every time so far. Usually 1 or 1+2 is enough.
